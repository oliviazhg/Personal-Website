The Intersection of Technology and Humanity

As we build more sophisticated digital experiences, I can't help but think about the human element. Technology should enhance our humanity, not replace it. When I create interactive art or build user interfaces, I'm not just writing code - I'm creating experiences that people will interact with, feel, and remember. The most successful digital experiences are the ones that feel human, even though they're built with code.

Maybe that's why I'm drawn to creative coding and interactive art. It's a way to inject humanity into technology, to create digital experiences that evoke emotion and connection. The code becomes a medium for human expression, just like paint or clay.
